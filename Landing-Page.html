Why Visit Kent?

Kents picturesque scenery wil leave you feeling Re-invigorated, Cultured and Inspired

Dover

With the towering dover castle proudly watching over the intriguing sites below . You are sure to find something that fits your taste, make sure you visit the dover docks. Being a working dock it is a beautiful site to watch mooring boats.

Canterbury

From the canterbury cathedral to the Marlowe theater. You are never too young to visit these beautiful and unique places, do you want to see Kents Cultured side? Canterbury is the place for you

Folkestone

Known for the harbour arm, this beautiful little town is one you will not forget. Take a walk through town and find one of the iconic Banksy arts hiding around, or enjoy an unforgettable evening on the harbor arm watching the sun set.

Ashford

Are shopping sprees more your thing? Then head on down to Ashford and visit the Ashford outlet. start to feel a little hungry? Then head on down to the food court where you will find major food brands with delicious smells beckoning you in.
